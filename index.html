ABOUT US


Heavenly Herbs Care is a formulator and supplier of superior quality dietary supplements. Located in Rohtak, India, the company was founded in 2013 and incorporated in the same year. Heavenly Herbs Care puts special emphasis on ensuring the quality of the raw material, each batch being subjected to quality analysis and, only upon QC approval, is it stocked in our warehouse. Heavenly Herbs Care manufactures a whole range of dietary supplements, including weight loss, anti-oxidants, diabetic control, sexual wellness, liver tonic, lipid balance and many more.
